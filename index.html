Privacy Policy

Impostor Football — Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

Impostor Football does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (player names, scores) is stored locally on your device and is cleared when you close the app.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Ads & Tracking

This app displays interstitial ads via Google AdMob. You may be asked for permission to track your activity across apps (App Tracking Transparency). No other analytics or tracking SDKs are used.

No Accounts

This app does not require or support user accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. No personal information is collected from any user, including children.

Disclaimer

Impostor Football is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any football club, league, player, or governing body. No player images, logos, or trademarked materials are used in this app.
